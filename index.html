Bikash Kunwar

I am a design engineer for drones at Prokura Innovations, a start-up working to deliver emergency medicines in rural parts of Nepal. I received my Bachelor in Mechanical Engineering in 2019 from Pulchowk Campus, Institute of Engineering, Tribhuvan University. I took aeronautics as elective in the final year and performed a group project on fixed-wing UAV.

I was born and raised in a village in Baglung, Nepal. I attended several schools progressively away from home that reminds me of a quote from the movie "The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey" "...but home is now behind you. The world is ahead."

Outside of academics, I like cycling, hiking, reading and writing.
